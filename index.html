Since 1989 Koby Environmental has been designing and manufacturing environmental groundwater testing and monitoring equipment. Our diverse line of locking well plugs and down hole well equipment is used by industry professionals worldwide. We use only the highest quality engineering grade virgin polymer blends in our products without substitution. We also offer custom design, mold building and injection molding services. We service a diverse line of customers discretely and chances are you have used a product that was manufactured by Koby. All of our products are manufactured in our Clarence, NY, USA facility without outsourcing of any kind. If you have any questions please let us know, we look forward to servicing your needs.
